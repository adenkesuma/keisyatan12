Our First Teamdays!

We went to the Selfie Time, Sun Plaza to take our first picture as a team. We decided the dresscode of this teamdays is black. It was fun, but unfortunately we missed one person that not be able to attend this teamdays, which is Vika as OCVP CR.

Execution Day 1 - Hearthpoly

This is us after held a campaign and played monopoly at the same time. We doubted ourself "Can we run the session smoothly?". But at the end, we ended this session with so much love and laugh from our participants. They also asked us to provide an extra time!

Execution Day 2 - Mangrove Action

We went to the Mangrove Kampoeng Nipah beach for our Execution Day 2. There so much fun because we can see the mudskipper everywhere! We should be careful while walking there. We planted the mangrove tree and took so many photos. I would like to go there again with the same people.

Farewell Party

This session made us feel blue, because our project is already over. Our participants cried when we played a throwback video compilation. As an OCs, we gave our last love message speech to our participants. We cried a lot because this experience is really meaningful for us. I just wish we not forget each other.

Space with OCs

We have so much topics to talk, we gave a sugarcubes for each other because this will be the last chance to said that. We are able to know each other perspective about ourself, and take notes for all the insight from each other.

Team Closing

This will be my hardest part as OCs, because we are no longer present as a team. We will have a different things to do, and maybe get to lost contact. We decided to take a picture for the last meet. I hope they have a great journey, whatever it is.
